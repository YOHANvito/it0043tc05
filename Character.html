Mikasa

Mikasa Ackerman is a central character in "Attack on Titan," renowned for her exceptional combat abilities and unwavering loyalty. From a young age, Mikasa demonstrated remarkable physical strength and agility, qualities that made her one of the most formidable soldiers in the series. Her skills were further honed after joining the Survey Corps, where she became a key member in humanity's fight against the Titans. Mikasa's combat prowess is often highlighted in battles, where her speed, precision, and tactical acumen set her apart from her peers.

Armin

Armin Arlert is a prominent character in "Attack on Titan," known for his intelligence, strategic mind, and unwavering determination. Despite his initial lack of physical strength, Armin's keen intellect and ability to devise innovative strategies make him an invaluable asset to the Survey Corps. His quick thinking and analytical skills have saved his comrades on numerous occasions, proving that his strengths lie beyond mere physical prowess. Armin's character evolves significantly throughout the series, as he overcomes his insecurities and rises to become a courageous leader, driven by his deep desire to uncover the truths of their world and ensure the survival of humanity.

Levi

Levi Ackerman, often regarded as humanity's strongest soldier, is a pivotal character in "Attack on Titan." His unparalleled combat skills, cold demeanor, and unwavering commitment to his mission set him apart as a formidable force against the Titans. As the captain of the elite Special Operations Squad, Levi is respected and feared by both allies and enemies. His precise and efficient fighting style, coupled with his strict discipline, often leads to decisive victories in battle. Despite his tough exterior, Levi harbors a deep sense of loss and compassion, particularly towards his fallen comrades, which adds depth to his character and drives his relentless pursuit of humanity's freedom.

Hanji

As the head of the Corps' research division and later its commander, Hange's innovative approach to understanding Titans has led to significant breakthroughs and strategic advantages. Their relentless pursuit of knowledge often borders on obsession, but it is driven by a genuine desire to protect humanity and uncover the mysteries of their world.
